Psychological Profile of Frank Costello

Biography:

Frank Costello was born on January 26, 1891. He was born in Iraly, and in 1895, he boarded a ship to the United States with his mother and his brother Edward to join their father in New York city.


He had become a member of local gang, and comitted to assault, robbery, and carrying a concealed weapon. He allied with different mafia groups, had gambling rackets, and became a crime boss nicknamed "The Prime Minister of the UnderWorld".

He became one of the most powerful and influential mafia bosses in american history, and Godfather movie was based on his life.

Nature Versus Nurture:

He had a father, a mother, and a brother Edward who introduced him to gang activities.
He was living with his mother and brother before he goes to America with them to join his father, so he was born as an immigrant in a poor family that opened a grocery store in their neighbourhood in New york.
Living in a hard situation, migrating at an early age, and being in a poor family made him to lose the concept of home, safety, and happiness, and that's why he became a member of local gang at age thirteen.
His family, childhood, and hardships made him a hopeless man who wanted to get rich by any crime. He became a perfect crime boss because he started gaining crime experiences from his early age, and also having a tough childhood made him help some groups of people.

Morality

Morality:

Frank Costello's morality level was self-interest because he was doing what was the best to satisfy himself. He didn't want to look good to others, he never cared about law (he made a system to pay judges, police officers and... to avoid trouble), and he never tried for a greater good morality.

Personality:

1. Openness- High/ He had many activities based on new ideas which led him to become the best in his business.

2. Conscientiousness- High/ He was dutiful in his crime actions, and tried hard in the competence between him and other crime organizations.

3. Extroversion- High/ He was always connected with a lot of people, excitement seeking about crime manners, and having positive emotions about his activities.

4. Agreeableness- Low/ He wasn't compatible with society.

5. Neuroticism- Low/ He was always relaxed and careless, and that was why he was doing crimes with no hard feelings.
